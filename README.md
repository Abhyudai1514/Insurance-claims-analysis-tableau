# 🚗 Vehicle Insurance Claims Analysis using Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a Car Insurance Claims dataset to identify the key factors driving insurance claims and financial risk. Using Tableau, I built interactive dashboards to explore customer behavior, household risk, vehicle risk, driving patterns, and financial exposure.

The goal is to help insurance companies make better underwriting, pricing, and risk management decisions through data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

✔ Which customers generate the highest claims?

✔ How do household characteristics impact risk?

✔ Which vehicle types are the riskiest?

✔ How do driving patterns influence claims?

✔ Which financial and geographic segments create the highest exposure?

✔ What actions can reduce claim costs and improve profitability?

---

---

## 📊 Dashboards

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of portfolio performance, claim rate, claim amount, and customer distribution.

### 2️⃣ Claim Analysis

Identifies customer segments contributing the most to claim frequency and severity.

### 3️⃣ Customer & Household Risk

Analyzes the impact of family structure, children, parent status, and income on claim behavior.

### 4️⃣ Vehicle & Driving Risk

Evaluates vehicle type, vehicle age, travel patterns, and young driver impact on claims.

### 5️⃣ Financial & Geographic Exposure

Examines income, home value, vehicle value, previous claims, and urbanicity to understand financial risk.

---

## 📖 Tableau Story Flow

Executive Overview ➜ Claim Analysis ➜ Customer Risk ➜ Vehicle Risk ➜ Financial Exposure ➜ Key Insights ➜ Recommendations

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

📌 38.3% of policyholders filed at least one claim.

📌 Claim costs are concentrated within a smaller group of customers.

📌 Households with multiple children and young drivers show higher claim risk.

📌 Panel Trucks emerged as the highest-risk vehicle category.

📌 Urban customers generate higher claim costs than rural customers.

📌 Previous claim history is a strong indicator of future risk.

📌 Income, home value, and vehicle value influence claim exposure.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

✅ Implement risk-based pricing for high-risk customer and vehicle segments.

✅ Reward claim-free customers through loyalty and safe-driving programs.

✅ Strengthen underwriting for households with young drivers.

✅ Use previous claim history more effectively during policy renewal.

✅ Develop targeted risk management strategies for urban customers.

✅ Monitor high-risk vehicle categories and driving behaviors.
